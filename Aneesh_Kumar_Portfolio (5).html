I worked with the Bhagwan Jagannath Foundation as a digital volunteer from 10 May to 10 June 2025. The foundation runs 10+ schools and a free hospital in the remote Himalayan villages of Uttarkashi — communities so off-grid that children trek 1–2 hours through mountain trails to reach class. Their work was real and impactful, but almost entirely invisible online.

THE PROBLEM
  • Google Maps listing had only 1 photo and no description — scaring off volunteers
  • Instagram username was long & forgettable; bio was unoptimized with no clear CTA
  • No content series, no consistent posting rhythm, very low reach
  • No system to acknowledge donors — causing one-time giving, no repeat support
MY ROLE

Built a complete 4-pillar digital strategy — shifting the foundation's online presence from "almost invisible" to "actively building trust and attracting support."

PILLAR I · LOCAL SEO
Google Maps Overhaul

Added description, multi-photo gallery, walkthrough video & complete contact info — turned listing into a trust-building mini-landing page.

PILLAR II · BRAND
Instagram Brand & Bio

Suggested memorable handle ("mountainsuntoldstories"), rewrote bio to lead with mission + CTA, added optimized single link — every visitor now understands the foundation in 5 seconds.

PILLAR III · CONTENT
"A Day in a Child's Life" Series

Designed an episodic storytelling format — influencer follows one child home after school, filming the trek, family & village. Repeatable, emotional, shareable. Also mapped local festivals for ongoing content pipeline.

PILLAR IV · CONVERSION
Donor Thank-You Reel System

When a donation arrived, filmed a short reel showing children receiving & using the gift — posted on Instagram & LinkedIn. Closed the trust loop, converting first-time donors into repeat supporters.

INFLUENCER STRATEGY

Built a vetting system for volunteer-influencers (follower count, engagement quality, content tone) with monthly on-site visit cadence. Every collab uploaded via Instagram's Collab Posts feature — doubling reach without duplicating effort.

HEADLINE RESULT
₹30,000+ in donations
Directly attributed to thank-you reels — within weeks of launch, donors who had never engaged before started contributing.
